Douglas Santos is a Principal Software Engineer and Solutions Architect with over 16 years of experience in designing and developing cloud-based applications, SaaS platforms, and distributed systems. Currently at BLIP, he leads architectural design of cloud applications while providing technical mentorship and ensuring secure development practices. His expertise spans microservices, event-driven architecture, and security implementation, including participation in ISO27001 audit processes.

With previous experience at Dell Technologies, Softise Systems, TOTVS, and other organizations, Douglas has consistently delivered innovative solutions across retail, financial services, and agribusiness sectors. His technical skills encompass .NET technologies, multiple database systems, DevOps tools (Azure DevOps, Docker, Kubernetes), and security protocols.

Douglas is committed to software quality through Clean Code principles, TDD, and Agile methodologies. His ability to align technical strategies with business objectives while maintaining high standards of security and scalability makes him an exceptional technology leader.
